Quickstart

  1. Build or buy your 8051 based hardware.
  2. Select or configure a copy of PAULMON2 for your hardware.
  3. Program the EPROM with PAULMON2.
  4. Run your terminal emulation program.
  5. Turn on your 8051 board and press Enter (for auto baud detection)
  6. Write an 8051 program, or copy one of the examples
  7. Download the Intel-Hex file ( 'D' command)
  8. Jump to your program ( 'J' command)
